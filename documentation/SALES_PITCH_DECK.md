   - Improves your cash flow
   - Example: $25k → $22.5k if paid 100% upfront

3. **Multiple Property Types Discount: 10%**
   - If they commit to multiple sites (residential + commercial)
   - Example: 2 sites at $25k each = $50k → $45k

4. **Referral Incentive: 10-15%**
   - If they refer another agency and they buy
   - Builds your client base
   - Example: Refer 2 agencies, get $3k-5k commission per sale

### **What NOT to Discount**
- ❌ Don't drop below $12k for full package (devalues your work)
- ❌ Don't discount more than 25% total
- ❌ Don't offer discounts without getting something in return (faster payment, referral, testimonial)

## Negotiation Framework

### **Your Walk-Away Price (Minimum Acceptable)**
- **Absolute Minimum:** $12,000 (covers your cost + 50% markup)
- **Target Range:** $20,000 - $35,000 (sweet spot for most clients)
- **Stretch Goal:** $50,000+ (enterprise clients)

### **Negotiation Tactics**

1. **Bundle Value (Don't Lower Price)**
   - Instead of dropping from $25k to $20k
   - Keep at $25k but add: "I'll include WhatsApp integration ($2.5k value) and extend support to 120 days ($3k value)"
   - Client feels they got a deal, you maintain price integrity

2. **Payment Terms Leverage**
   - Offer flexible payment: 50% upfront, 25% at midpoint, 25% at launch
   - Or: Monthly payment plan ($5k down, $1.5k/month for 12 months = $23k total)
   - Higher total price, but easier for client to budget

3. **Scope Reduction (Last Resort)**
   - If they can't afford $25k: "What if we start with 5 core pages ($15k) and add the rest later?"
   - Maintains relationship, gets foot in door, upsell opportunity later

4. **Exclusivity Premium**
   - "For $30k, I'll guarantee no other agency in [City] gets this platform for 12 months"
   - Competitive advantage justifies higher price
