Our Story

Founded in 2018, Umanie Homes Africa emerged from a vision to transform the luxury real estate landscape across the African continent. We recognized a gap in the market for a premium, full-service real estate firm that could match the sophistication of global luxury property brands while celebrating Africa's unique architectural heritage and emerging cosmopolitan excellence.

Today, we are proud to be Africa's premier luxury real estate destination, with a carefully curated portfolio of exceptional properties across the continent's most prestigious addresses. From contemporary penthouses in Lagos to elegant villas in Cape Town, we connect discerning clients with homes that reflect their aspirations and lifestyle.

Our success is built on unwavering commitment to excellence, deep market expertise, and personalized service that goes beyond transactions to create lasting relationships with our clients.

Our Mission

To deliver unparalleled luxury real estate experiences that exceed expectations, connecting visionary clients with exceptional properties across Africa while fostering sustainable communities and economic growth.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected luxury real estate brand in Africa, renowned for integrity, innovation, and our commitment to showcasing the continent's architectural excellence to the world.
